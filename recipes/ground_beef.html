Ground Beef

Description

Grass Fed Beef

  • 1 pound 85/15 grass fed ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon grass fed beef tallow
  • Pink Salt

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron pan to 7/10 heat
  2. Shape 1 pound beef to two 8 ounce patties
  3. Salt appropriately
  4. Cook for 3 minutes on first side and 1.5 minutes on second side
  5. Enjoy
