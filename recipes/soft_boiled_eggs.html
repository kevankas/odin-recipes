Description

Soft Boiled Eggs

  • Eggs
  • A pot 75% full of water

Steps

  1. Boil the water in the pot
  2. Lower the heat to 4 and add the eggs in the pot
  3. Raise the heat back to the max setting
  4. Set a timer for 6 minutes and 30 seconds and remove the eggs after time is up
  5. Immediately remove the eggs from the heat and place them under cool water or an ice bath
  6. Enjoy
